Course and Group Introduction

Group 9 is made up of Level 200 first-year students specializing in Web Development and this course is termed as Web Development 1. This page showcases our course objectives, introduces each group member, and highlights our shared passion for building meaningful digital products.

Web Development I Objectives

  1. Introduce students to the fundamental concepts of how the Internet and the World Wide Web function.
  2. Explain the key web protocols (HTTP/HTTPS), terminologies, and the phases of the web development lifecycle.
  3. Help students identify and understand the purpose of core HTML5 elements and tags.
  4. Lead students to construct valid, semantically structured web pages using HTML5.
  5. Lead students to apply CSS3 to effectively control the layout, typography, color, and overall visual presentation of web pages.
  6. Enable students to host a website using available Web hosting services
